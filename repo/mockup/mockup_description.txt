## Mockup
New version of the calculator will be in the same minimalistic design but there will be many new functions.
First of all, menu bar will be added and the main feature of menu bar is the converter
which will be able to convert time, currency, speed etc. Also, Help button was added, on the click
of user manual will be opened. In converter you can choose 'source' and destination value, the number
that is currently in the main label will be converted.
Secondly, PRO mode will have memory stuck where you can store interim results of your calculation 'M+'
and then pop them 'M-', also you can clear the whole stuck with 'MC' button.
And finally, PRO mode will enable you to draw graphs in the separate window.
